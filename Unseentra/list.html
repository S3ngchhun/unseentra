Cambodia

Cambodia's geography has played a substantial role in shaping its history. Its central location along the Indochinese Peninsula and relatively flat terrain facilitated the rise of the Khmer Empire which flourished from its base in Angkor for over 500 years.

About Cambodia

Cambodia Tourism is about tropical beaches, historical monuments, lively nightlife, and beautiful countryside. Due to its moderate tropical weather, Cambodia has been attracting thousands of tourists every year. During travel to Cambodia, experiences some of its best days in the cold and dry months from November to February. It is the peak tourist season in Cambodia when the temperature goes down to 20°C making the Cambodia trip experience very pleasant and exciting for the tourists. The city of Phnom Penh, the capital of Cambodia, is a fine specimen of the country's contemporary culture.


People and Religion in Cambodia

With 97% of followers, Buddhism is the main religion in the country. The history of the country also plays an important role in developing Cambodia Tourism industry, because people visit the place to take a look at the marvelous architecture of various buildings and temples that were built during the 12th century.

Being a beautiful country in South East Asia, there are many Cambodia tourist attractions and Cambodia things to do which people must take note of while visiting this country.
